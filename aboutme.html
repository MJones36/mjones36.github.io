Martha Jones is a creative, curious and meticulous data analyst who is passionate about civic tech.

Martha's love for advocacy and social justice began at Temple University where she earned degrees in Rhetoric & Public Advocacy and Sociology. During her time at Temple, Martha was introduced to the data collection and analysis process which would soon become her passion.

After graduation, Martha's passion for analyzing data and understanding the world grew even stronger. She entered the tech and analytics industry where she became well-versed in programming languages such as Python, R, and SQL. Upon teaching herself HTML and CSS, Martha built her own website, and Advocacy and Analytics was born.

The mission for Advocacy and Analytics is to bridge the gap between social justice and analytics by providing resources for advocates, analysts and data scientists eager to use the intersection of technology, social justice, and data analytics to strengthen their cause.
